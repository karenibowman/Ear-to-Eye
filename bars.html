ABOUT THIS DISPLAY This display is a representation of a fourier transform - a function of time in regard the frequencies registered in sound. Typically, sound is represented as one continous wave. A fourier transform, or "frequency represention function," is a collecion of how frequently certain sized "peaks" appear in the sound detected. The bars on the left are associated with smaller peaks, the ones on the right with lower peaks. If you try singing a pitch, can you get a steady peak to form on the graph?
