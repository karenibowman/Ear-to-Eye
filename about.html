AboutThe purpose of Ear to Eye is to help you explore sound.

Most people pay attention to visuals over sounds. Sure, we all listen to music and some of us enjoy playing instruments, but street noise and other everyday sounds don't catch our attention. I'm convinced there is value in all sounds, even the small sounds we make on the daily. Explore different menu items to experience sound in new ways!
